LEARN TO DESTIGMATIZE

When it comes to mental health, many people like to avoid the seriousness of the topic and assume that if they do care, they will be perceived as "too weak" and "too dramatic." If you or someone you know are ever told this, remember that your feelings are valid and you should never minimize the situation because your wellness comes first. The internet has become more accessible allowing us to do more research. Be aware of the sites you are looking at because some may contain misleading information (use .edu and .gov websites). The best way to destigmatize is to talk to a professional or trusted loved one about your feelings; this allows you to be more comfortable about the topic of mental health. Lastly, if you see something online that would be helpful to others, think about posting/sharing it to spread awareness.

LEARN ABOUT DISORDERS

01

ANXIETY DISORDERS

OVERVIEW

Anxiety disorders happens when a person experiences continous anxiety throughout the day, which can interfere with daily actvities. Some of the types of anxiety disorders include generalized anxiety, panic, social anxiety, and phobia-related anxiety. Some symptoms include nervousness, panic, sweating, and rapid heartbeat. Anxiety disorders can be caused trauma, stress, other mental disorders, etc. Treatment includes therapy, medication, support groups, and etc.

02

DEPRESSION

OVERVIEW

Depression is a mood disorder that can affect common daily activities. Some of the types are major, persistent depressive, perinatal, seasonal affective, and depression with symptoms of psychosis. The symptoms include decreased energy, difficulty concentrating, difficulty sleeping, changes in appetite, headaches, and more. Treatment can include therapy, medication, and social groups.

03

PERSONALITY DISORDERS

OVERVIEW

Personality disorders are patterns of abnormal behavior from a person, which can be caused by trauma. They affect way of thinking, responding emotionally, relating to other people, and controlling a person's behavior. Some types are antisocial, borderline, dependent, narcissistic, and more. Treatment includes different types of therapy and psychoeducation.

Learn More Crisis Hotline

04

POST-TRAUMATIC STRESS DISORDER

OVERVIEW

Post- traumatic stress disorder is caused by a traumatic event. The symptoms include flashbacks, uncontrollable thoughts, and severe anxiety. This disorder can change the way of thinking and mood, as well as physical and emotional reactions. Treatment includes psychotherapy and medication.

05

EATING DISORDERS

OVERVIEW

Eating disorders are disturbances in people's eating because of doubtful thoughts and emotions. Two types of eating disorders are anorexia nervosa and bulimia nervosa, but there are many more. Symptoms can vary through eating small amounts of food or certain food to eating a lot of food and then throwing it up. Treatment includes psychotherapy and medication.
